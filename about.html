The Founder's Journey

Mr. Maruti Yedge

Chairman & Managing Director, My Bhoomi Group

My Bhoomi Group was conceptualized on a simple but revolutionary philosophy: high-yield land investment should be structured with the same legal exactness and structural quality as premium skyscrapers.

Holding a Civil Engineering degree alongside an MBA in Human Resources, I spent my early career analyzing infrastructure models. I realized that while land appreciation in Pune growth corridors was immense, the plotting sector was plagued by title disputes, undocumented boundaries, and subpar development.

Under My Bhoomi Realty, we set out to build a platform that guarantees absolute safety. Today, with over 200 projects developed and 500+ happy clients, we stand as Western Maharashtra's premier developer of MahaRERA-certified NA plots and curated organic farm layouts.

State Level Accolade

Recipients of "Maharashtrache Anmol Ratna" 2023

We are deeply honored to have been officially recognized at the inaugural "Maharashtrache Anmol Ratna" awards, organized by the prominent news network ABP Majha.

This award celebrates outstanding organizations and individuals who have contributed heavily to the development and socio-economic progress of Maharashtra. The recognition reflects our strict adherence to customer trust, structural engineering compliance, and transparent corporate governance.

Social Commitment

Balaji Education Society

Founded by Mr. Maruti Yedge in 2008, establishing a pathway of hope, education, and financial relief for promising students across rural Maharashtra.

Empowering Through Education

We believe that corporate success is hollow without community elevation. The Balaji Education Society operates as the philanthropic heart of My Bhoomi Group.

For over 18 years, the foundation has identified brilliant, underprivileged students from agricultural households, providing them with complete financial scholarships, technical resources, and professional mentorship to build career paths in civil sciences, engineering, and digital systems.
