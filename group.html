The Mybhoomi Group

A diversified conglomerate spanning real estate, hospitality, industrial engineering, agro-farming, media, and education across Western Maharashtra.

Group Legacy

Corporate Synergies & Multi-Sector Stability

Mybhoomi Group stands as one of Western Maharashtra's most resilient and diversified business houses. What started as an engineering-driven land planning initiative by Maruti Yedge has evolved into a strategic conglomerate of 12 distinct brands.

By integrating our own raw material supply, in-house heavy civil machinery, specialized legal boundary survey practices, and corporate media wings, we achieve unmatched operational speed, legal security, and cost efficiency. This unified network translates directly into institutional-grade protection and premium value for our land investors.
